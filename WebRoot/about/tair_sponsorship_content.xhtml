TAIR Corporate Sponsorship Program

Dear TAIR supporter,

As you may have heard, after ten years of grant support from the National Science Foundation, funding for the Arabidopsis Information Resource (TAIR) is to be phased out over the next three years ( http://www.arabidopsis.org/doc/about/tair_funding/410). This resource, which is used by 38,000 scientists, teachers and students worldwide every month, is critically important to plant scientists in both the public and private sectors and must be maintained.

In order to continue providing this essential service, we are establishing a new corporate sponsorship program to help support TAIR. We strongly feel that such a sponsorship program is preferable to implementing a subscription requirement for the private sector because it will allow us to keep TAIR open and free of login requirements, facilitating the free exploration of data by all scientists.

We hope that you will join us in keeping this essential resource available to plant scientists in both the public and private sectors.
